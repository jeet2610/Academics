Sarvajanik College of Engineering & Technology is a self financed institute offering Under-graduate degree in Engineering Courses & Architecture.

It was established in 1995. The institute is recognized by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and affiliated to Veer Narmad South Gujarat University.
Sarvajanik College Of Engineering & Technology (SCET) is situated in the historical place of Surat.
